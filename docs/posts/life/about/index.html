Who I am

I grew up as a rich kid in Long Island, New York. After graduating high school, I went to Purdue University and realized that the education system shouldn't be the thing that determines whether a person lives a life of privilege and abundance or not, so here we are. I'm not 100% sure how I'm going to do it, but I know that if I can just help even one person elevate their socioeconomic status with technology, I'll be happy.
